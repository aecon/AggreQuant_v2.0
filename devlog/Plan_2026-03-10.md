## Preprocessing Cellpose Segmentation Benchmark

New benchmark: quantify how different preprocessing of the nuclear channel affects
Cellpose cyto3 cell segmentation. Three variants: cell-only, raw DAPI, StarDist
binary nuclei seeds. Full plan in `benchmarks/preprocessing_cellpose_segmentation/BENCHMARK_PLAN.md`.
